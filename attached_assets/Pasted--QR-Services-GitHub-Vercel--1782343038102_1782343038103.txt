## 6) Production Gate

حدّث production-hardening-gate ليضيف checks لصفحة QR:

* QR page exists.
* QR route exists.
* QR Services appears in navigation.
* QR Services appears in footer.
* QR generator function exists.
* WhatsApp QR exists.
* Request Delivery QR exists.
* Contact QR exists.
* Download QR action exists.
* No camera permission break if scanner not enabled.

ثم نفّذ:

pnpm run build
pnpm run production:gate

يجب أن تكون النتيجة PASS بالكامل.
