# DAY NIGHT Merchant Business Center

The `/merchant` portal is a bilingual, responsive workspace built for approved DAY NIGHT merchants.

## Operational surfaces

- Overview with real order, delivery, and COD metrics
- Merchant service center for new orders and coupon photo intake
- Searchable and filterable order management
- Live shipment and courier tracking through the shared production map
- COD, collected amount, merchant due, delivered value, and settlement summaries
- Merchant logo, business identity, contact, pickup, license, and TRN management
- Direct 24/7 operations support

The portal preserves the existing Supabase authentication, merchant RPCs, realtime subscriptions, profile update RPC, and direct-query fallbacks. No mock operational records are used.
